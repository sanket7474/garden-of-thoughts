This series is about my thoughts.  
A quote, a line, something that made me stop and think.  
Written exactly how I'd write it in my diary.  
No filter. Just honest.

<Quote center author="Michael Rosenberg">
You Only Miss The Sun When It Starts To Snow.
</Quote>


When things are always around us  
we rarely pause to notice.  
Their presence feels ordinary  
until their absence speaks.  

The people we see every day  
the moments we barely notice.  
The time we think we have  
the days we call ordinary.  
The smiles we see so often  
the voices that fill our days.  

Only when they are missing,  
we start to understand.  
What felt ordinary yesterday  
was quietly precious all along.  

So take a moment today,  
to notice what still remains.  
Some things are meant to be valued  
before the snow arrives.  

## Remember,  
### Notice and appreciate what you have today.    
### Not everything we love stays forever.
