<Quote center>
Never Cry Over Spilled Milk
</Quote>

Not everything you lose is a mistake.  
Not everything that leaves, was in your control.  

Sometimes it wasn't your choice,  
Sometimes it's plan that didn't work,  
Sometimes a moment gets missed,  
Sometimes life chooses different directions for you,  
Sometimes effort doesn't guarantee outcome,  
Sometimes the answer is no, even after trying,  
Sometimes the timing just isn't right,  
Sometimes life moves differently than expected,  
Sometimes you did everything you could, and still things slipped away.  

## And That's Okay!  

What's lost isn't always taken from you to hurt you.  
It's removed because it no longer belongs to the life you're growing into.  

You don't cry over spilled milk,  
because it was never meant to stay in the cup forever.  
It spilled because it had already served it's purpose....  

---

## Trust This -  

If something left your life without your choice,  
It was meant to.  
And it left to make space for something better.  

### So Take A Breath.... & Let Go.  

What's meant to stay will never spill.  
And whatever spilled made space for something better.
