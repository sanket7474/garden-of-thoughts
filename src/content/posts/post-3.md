<p>When things are always around us <br>
we rarely pause to notice. <br>
Their presence feels ordinary <br>
until their absence speaks. <br>
</p> 
<br>
<p>
The people we see every day<br>
the moments we barely notice. <br>
The time we think we have<br>
the days we call ordinary.<br>
The smiles we see so often<br>
the voices that fill our days. <br>
</p>
<br>
<p>
Only when they are missing<br>
we start to understand.<br>
What felt ordinary yesterday<br>
was quietly precious all along. <br>
</p>
<br>
<p>
So take a moment today<br>
to notice what still remains.<br> 
Some things are meant to be valued<br> 
before the snow arrives. <br>
<p>

## So,

### Notice and appreciate what you have today. 
### Not everything we love     stays forever.
