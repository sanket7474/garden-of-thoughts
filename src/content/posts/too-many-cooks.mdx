This series is about my thoughts.  
A quote, a line, something that made me stop and think.  
Written exactly how I'd write it in my diary.  
No filter. Just honest.

<Quote center author="John Hooker">
Too Many Cooks Spoil The Dish
</Quote>

We are constantly surrounded by openions, advice and expectations.  
Sometimes it feels like each voice pulls us in different direction,  
and then the plan we hoped to follow begin to blur or fade.

Life's decision demand focus, reflections and courage to pause.  
When we stop trying to include every suggestion,  
we dicover a quite stillness inside, that guides the way forward.

Listening to yourself isnt selfish, it is where clarity begins.  
Too often, we let our fear or doubt give power to the voices of others.  

We try to balance or consider every suggetion, every openion    
and in doing so we forgot the simplest tuth:  
`We Are The Once Living This Life`

Not every piece of advice carries meaning for us,  
and not every expectation belongs on our shoulders.

<u>Choose you own way, because too mamy cooks spil the dish.</u>
